About Us

St. Joseph’s Institute of Management (SJIM)

St. Joseph’s Institute of Management (SJIM), Bangalore is an ever-evolving center of education and invention. Started in 1968 as offshoot of St. Joseph’s College of Commerce (SJCC) at Brigade Road, SJIM found itself a growing and reshaping itself along with other Jesuit-run St Joseph’s Colleges and Institutions in Bangalore. It renamed from St. Joseph’s College of Business Administration into St Joseph’s Institute of Management (SJIM) with an independent campus in the heart of the Garden City, M.G.Road, Bangalore. Approved by AICTE in 1996 for a Two Year Full-Time PGDM, accredited by NBA, is a member of Global Jesuits Network International Association of Jesuit Business Schools (IAJBS) and Xavier Association of Management Institutions (XAMI).

Vision

Eligibility

  • Graduate in BE/BTech/BCom/BBA/BSc/BA or any other bachelors degree from a recognized university under regular mode only.
  • The candidate should have a consistent academic record of the first class (minimum 60% marks) in STD X, STD XII & Graduation.
  • The candidates who have cleared all the subjects in their first attempt will be preferred. Candidates with a history of more than two arrears need not apply. Candidates who have appeared for the Final Graduate Level examination and are awaiting results may also apply.

Finance Marketing HR Operations Analytics

Entrance Test Scores

  • CAT 2024: 60 percentile or above
  • XAT 2025: 60 percentile or above
  • MAT (Sep 2024, Dec 2024, Feb 2025, May 2025): A minimum of 550 Composite Score.
  • We also accept GMAT, ATMA and CMAT scores (60 percentile or above)

Application & Shortlisting

Download Admission Prospectus from the website. Make an online payment of Rs 1,300 for application. Applicants will be shortlisted based on consistent academic performance, Entrance Test score, and work experience. The list of shortlisted candidates will be put up on the SJIM website for Personal Interview and English language test conducted online or offline. The individual candidates will also be informed through e-mail or SMS.

Industry Connect

At St. Joseph's Institute of Management (SJIM), Bengaluru, we recognize the paramount importance of bridging the gap between academia and industry. Our Industry-Connect programs serve as a conduit for fostering meaningful collaborations between the academic community and various industries. By facilitating partnerships through Memorandums of Understanding (MOUs), SJIM endeavors to create mutually beneficial relationships that drive innovation, research, and experiential learning.

Through our Industry-Connect initiative, we seek to establish robust channels for knowledge exchange, skill development, and real-world problem-solving. By forging alliances with industries and institutions sharing similar goals and interests, we aim to enrich our academic programs, enhance student learning experiences, and address contemporary challenges faced by the industry.

Catalyst Centre

Catalyst Centre is the innovation and entrepreneurship hub of St. Joseph’s Institute of Management (SJIM). It nurtures entrepreneurial thinking, supports student-led startups, and empowers next-generation leaders through tiered programs, mentoring, and incubation support. From idea discovery to venture scaling, Catalyst provides a dynamic platform for students, alumni, and family business successors. Rooted in Jesuit values and driven by impact, the Centre is where vision meets action.

Vision & Mission

Vision: Increase the success rate of startups in India, positively impacting the economy with more wealth creation for stakeholders.

Mission: Provide end to end support to innovative products and productized services to achieve positive disruption in the markets & society.
